Company

Profile

ION, Inc. offers a broad spectrum of business and security solutions to meet the needs of a wide range of international clients, tailoring our abilities to special environments and requiremens. ION is positioned as the largest US-based special service contractor, and a major player on the global scene. With an established company headquarters in South Carolina, USA, you know we are a reliable force - here for the long term.

Philosophy

Integrity and discretion lie at the beating heart of ION, Inc. When we offer a service, expect no shortcuts nor sudden loss of support. Ninety percent of our clients rated our services as "outstanding" in the community feedback we gathered last fiscal year. Trust and accountability are our core business practices - drawing upon the strengths of major investors and shareholders around the world.

History

Established initially as a private aviation security agency, ION has expanded its capabilities further into the security services market, growing in size and reputation. ION is now the largest US-based company and is a major player on the global scene. Since being founded in 1998, ION has performed over 19,850 successful contracts - working with civilians and professionals from all over the globe.
